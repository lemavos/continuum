# Continuum

Continuum is a SaaS designed for people building things — personal projects, ideas, skills, and goals — who want to understand how their time, actions, and thoughts connect over time.

Instead of splitting everything into tasks, notes, or trackers, Continuum is built around a single concept: **entities**.

An entity can be anything:
a project, a topic, a skill, a person, or even something you're working towards.

---

## Core Idea

Continuum doesn’t start with structure.

It starts with usage.

You create entities as you go, and connections emerge naturally from what you do — not from systems you have to configure upfront.

Over time, this forms a lightweight graph of your work, learning, and daily activity.

---

## What you can do

- Track time spent on projects and activities  
- Log actions and progress without rigid systems  
- Connect entities through real usage, not manual setup  
- Keep context (time, notes, relationships) in one place  
- See how things evolve over time  

---

## Why Continuum

Most tools force you to choose:

- notes  
- tasks  
- trackers  
- graphs  

Continuum combines them into a single system where:

> your actions, context, and ideas live together

No switching between apps.  
No complex setup.  
No rigid methodology.

---

## Vision

Continuum is a living system.

Not something you organize once and maintain forever,  
but something that grows naturally as you use it.

A map of what you do, what you learn, and how everything connects over time.

Built for people who want clarity without friction.
